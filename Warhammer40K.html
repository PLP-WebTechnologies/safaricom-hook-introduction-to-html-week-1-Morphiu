INTRODUCTION: WHAT IS WARHAMMER 40K?

Warhammer 40K is a fictional universe that tries to depict what advancements humanity will have
made 38,000 years from now. Advancements such as space travel, surgeries that can allow you to lift
tonnes of heavy equipment and mobile exoskeletons all in the name of spreading your religion across
the galaxy.

It is the 41st Millennium. For more than a hundred centuries, the emperor has sat immobile on the golden
throne of earth. He is the master of mankind by the will of the gods, and master of a million worlds by the might
of his inexhaustible armies. He is a rotting carcass writhing invisibly with power from Dark Age technology.

He is the carrion lord of the imperium for whom a thousand souls are sacrificed today, so that he may never truly die.
To be a man in such times is to be amongst untold billions. It is to live in the cruelest and most bloody regime imaginable.
Forget the power of technology and science for so much has been forgotten, never to be relearned.
Forget the promise of progress and understanding, for in the dark future, there is only war.
